SHIPPING INFORMATION

Quick & free Shipping in US & Canada

When you place an order with us, we’ll make sure it gets to you in the US or Canada as fast as possible. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Order Processing: After you order, it takes up to 48 hours to verify, process, and ship it. We do this on business days (Monday – Friday).
  • Dispatch Time: We aim to send your order from our facility within 1-2 business days. But during busy times, like the year-end or sales events, it might take a bit longer.
  • Delivery Time in the US: Once we dispatch your order, it typically takes 12-20 business days to reach you. Keep in mind that this may vary during holidays or special launches.
  • Free Standard Shipping: We offer free standard shipping for all domestic US and Canada orders. It takes 12 to 20 business days.
  • Reliable Carriers: We use trusted carriers like DHL, FedEx, USPS, and UPS to make sure your order arrives safely.
  • Delivery Date Info: During checkout, we’ll let you know when to expect your delivery. Remember, we only process shipments on US business days.
  • Tracking: You’ll receive an email with your tracking number within 3-5 business days after your shipment is handed over to the carrier. You can use this number to track your order.
