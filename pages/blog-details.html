Fashion trends come and go, but certain pieces remain timeless, and the hoodie is a prime example. Storing Your Hoodie

Proper storage is critical to maintaining the quality of your hoodie. When not used, hang your hoodie on a sturdy hanger to prevent wrinkles and keep its shape.
